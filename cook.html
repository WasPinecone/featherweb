found some small fish near the water path and a bunch of crunchy bugs in the rocks, thought why not combine them into one meal

here’s my “adult Avian survival gourmet” recipe LULZ!!

  • Ingredients:
    • 5-6 small fish (fresh is best)
    • A handful of crunchy bugs (grasshoppers, beetles, whatever)
    • Some moss or edible herbs for seasoning
    • A splash of water for steaming
  • Instructions:
    • Clean the fish carefully, remove scales if needed (watch claws, knives are scary!)
    • Rinse the bugs in water, pick out the gross ones (legs sticking out too much LOL)
    • Heat a flat rock over fire (or sun if available), place fish and bugs on it
    • Sprinkle moss/herbs on top for flavor
    • Cover with a bigger rock and steam with a little water for ~10 minutes
    • Check if the bugs are crunchy and the fish is cooked (wiggly tails = raw!)
    • Serve on a leaf plate, eat immediately, enjoy the crunch + flavor combo

pro tip: never cook near other Avians unless you want them begging for a bite LULZ!!
